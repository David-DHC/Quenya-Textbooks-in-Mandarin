            • 不规则词形表（附录）

            I. 词干变换

            注释：下列词语，但凡需要添加后缀，则一律使用冒号后的形式添加后缀。若需要先添加元音再接后缀，则一律添加括号里的元音。例如：ambar "doom" → ambartanen "by doom"。

            • talan "floor": talam-
            • ambar "doom": ambart(a)-
            • nís "woman": niss-
            • firit "bird": firic-
            • feren "beech": fern-
            • falma "foam": falmal-

             

            II. 不规则过去式

            • hyar "to plough" → hyandë "ploughed"
            • ser "to rest"→sendë "rested"
            • lelya "to travel" → lendë "travelled"
            • papa "to tremble" → pampë "trembled"
            • pata "to tap"→pantë "tapped"
            • ëa "to exist"→engë "existed"
            • lahta "to cross, to surpass" → lahantë "crossed, surpassed"
            • nir "to press, to force" → nindë "pressed, forced"
            • lav "to lick" → lávë "licked"
            • car "to make" → cárë "made"
            • tul "to come" → túlë "came"

            III. 不规则完成式

            • fir "to die" → fírië "have died"

            IV. 不规则所有格

            • feren "beech" → ferenwa "of beech"
